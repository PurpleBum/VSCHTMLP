Nigerian Jollof Recipe

Nigerian Jollof Rice is an age old recipe eaten and enjoyed throughout Nigerians and other ethnicity's all over the world. It is a recipe that uses a combination of rice, tomatos, peppers, onions and spices to create a healthy and tasty dish. The necessary ingredients are listed below.

  • Sauteed Onions
  • Ginger and Garlic
  • Blended Peppers
  • Spices: Thyme, Curry Powder, Salt, Seasoning Cubes
  • Washed Rice and Chicken Stock
  • Oil

Steps

  1. First, saute the onions for three to five minutes.
  2. Once the onions are sauteed to your liking stir in tomato paste and fry for around five minutes.
  3. After the five minutes have passed add ginger and garlic. Cook for an additional two minutes.
  4. Add in the blended peppers. Fry until your desired thickness and the added oil. This process can take up to twenty to twenty-five minutes.
  5. As the sauce reaches your thickness taste add in the spices.
  6. Taste the sauce and confirm the seasoning is adequate. Once confirmed add in your washed rice and chicken stock.
  7. Reduce the heat once the rice comes to a boil and cook until finished.
