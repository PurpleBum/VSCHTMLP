Nigerian Egusi Stew is a wonderful spicy traditional Nigerian dish served with eba, pounded yam, fufu, and many other dishes.

Nigerian Egusi Stew Steps

  • Blended Onions
  • Palm oil
  • Egusi
  • Une
  • Stock
  • Spices
  • Ground Crayfish
  • Cooked meat & Fish
  • Washed bitter leaf and cut waterleaf

Nigerian Egusi Stew Recipe

  1. Prepare the egusi paste by blending the egusi seeds and onion mixture. Then set aside.
  2. In a large pot heat the palm oil and add the Une.
  3. Add the stock once the oil is hot and allow it to simmer
  4. Scoop a spoonful of the egusi paste into the stock.
  5. Simmer for 20-30 minutes then add assorted meat and fish.
  6. Add sliced pumpkin leaves and waterleaf
  7. Stir and let cook for 7 to 10 minutes and the leaves wilt.
  8. Add bitter leaf and leave the pot open while cooking for another 5 to 10 minutes
  9. Stir and adjust seasoning to suite your taste.
